About Me

Welcome to my practice. I believe that everyone deserves a space where they can be heard, understood, and supported without judgment. My approach to therapy is rooted in compassion, evidence-based practices, and the understanding that each person's journey is unique.

With over 12 years of experience in clinical psychology, I have had the privilege of working with individuals from all walks of life, helping them navigate through life's challenges and discover their inner strength. My therapeutic style combines cognitive-behavioral techniques with mindfulness-based approaches, creating a holistic framework for healing.

I am passionate about creating a warm, safe environment where you can explore your thoughts and feelings openly. Together, we'll work towards understanding patterns, developing coping strategies, and fostering personal growth that leads to lasting positive change.
